Data deletion instructions

Last updated August 15, 2023

This instruction is intended to explain to you how to delete your login information, personal information, and any data generated by using Koalang - AI English Teacher.

Please follow these steps:

  1. Download the latest version of Koalang - AI English Teacher app from your device's application store.
  2. Log in to the application using the account you wish to delete.
  3. On the Profile page, slide to the bottom.
  4. Click the Delete My Data button and confirm.

Be Careful: After completing the above operations, we will immediately erase all your data and usage records.
